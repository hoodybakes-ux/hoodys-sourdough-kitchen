Meet your new best friend
DoUGHLY
She's dehydrated. You're here to send help.
DoUGHLY is a dehydrated sourdough starter with serious personality. A few days of feeding and she'll be bubbling away, ready to raise your best loaves yet.
Reviving DoUGHLY

Bringing her back takes about 3 to 5 days. All you need is flour, water, a jar, and a little patience.

1
Day 1: Wake her up
Empty the DoUGHLY sachet into a clean jar. Add 30g of lukewarm water and stir until dissolved. Add 30g of flour, mix well, cover loosely, and leave somewhere warm (around 22 to 26°C).
2
Day 2: First feed
You may see a few small bubbles, or nothing yet. Both are normal. Add 30g flour and 30g water, stir, cover loosely, and leave in the same warm spot.
3
Days 3 to 4: Build her strength
Bubbles should be appearing now. Discard half the starter, then feed with 50g flour and 50g water. Repeat every 12 to 24 hours. She should start rising noticeably between feeds.
4
Day 5: She's ready
When she doubles within 4 to 8 hours of feeding with a domed top, bubbles throughout, and a jiggly texture, she's ready to bake with. Welcome to the sourdough life.
Hoody's tip: Skip the float test. Look for a domed top, bubbles throughout, and a jiggly texture instead. Those visual cues never lie.
